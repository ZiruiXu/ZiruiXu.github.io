Multivariable Calculus for Engineers and Applied Scientists

Course number: APMA E2000.

Semester: spring 2024.

Description: differential and integral calculus of multiple variables. Topics include partial differentiation; optimization of functions of several variables; line, area, volume, and surface integrals; vector functions and vector calculus; theorems of Green, Gauss, and Stokes; applications to selected problems in engineering and applied science.
